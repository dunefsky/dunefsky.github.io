Montgomery Scholars

I am a Montgomery Scholar which means I completed the two year honors program at Montgomery College, a community college in Rockville MD. The program was interdisciplinary and had a creative approach to the classroom. During my first year, every Tuesday and Thursday I would join 24 other students in one classroom with four professors teaching philosophy, history, literature, and world music. Later in the afternoon, we would continue our lessons in a writing class (fall) and an anthrology class (spring). In the second year, we enrolled in capstone, communications, and political science to write a sort of honors thesis. The program culminated in presenting at Beacon, an annual conference intended for community college students to come together and showcase their work. The following page is a culmination and nostalgia for my time in the Montgomery Scholars.
